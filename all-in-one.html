Artisan Made

brush The Craftsmanship

Each truffle is hand-painted by our master chocolatiers in Kyoto. We use a 72-hour conching process to ensure the texture is impeccably smooth, melting instantly upon contact with the palate to release the vibrant matcha aroma.

Ingredients & Allergens expand_more

Cacao beans, cane sugar, cocoa butter, whole milk powder, matcha powder, yuzu peel, soy lecithin, vanilla beans. Contains milk and soy. May contain traces of nuts.
